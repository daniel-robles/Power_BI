<!-- # Lesson 8.2 - DAX 2 -->

### Lesson Overview

<br>

The purpose of this lesson is to get familiarized with more DAX functions and equip students with skills to perform advanced calculations using Power BI.

<br><br>

### Learning Objectives

<br>

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

- apply calculated measures and `CALCULATE` DAX function to perform calculations in Power BI.

<br><br>
